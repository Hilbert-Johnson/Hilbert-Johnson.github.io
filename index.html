Siheng Zhao

Greetings! I’m a Junior now at School of Artificial Intelligence, Nanjing University. In this semester, i.e. 23spring, I'm a fully funded exchange student at Department of Computer Science, National University of Singapore.

Nowadays I work close with Prof. Lin Shao and do research related to Robotics. Previously I was a Research Intern at NJU NLP Group, advised by Prof. Shu-Jian Huang. My research interest now mainly lies at Robotics Learning especially Cloth Manipulation, Differentiable Simulation, 3D-aware Representation Learning, and the intersection between Robotics and Language.

I will join HKU NLP Group as Research Assistant this summer and work on LLM4Robotics with Prof. Tao Yu. Look forward to meeting you guys in Hong Kong!
